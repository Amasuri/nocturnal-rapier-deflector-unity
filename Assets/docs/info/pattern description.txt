===First Battle===
Easiest pattern. Codename is DIFFERENTIATING (not random, that would be hard)
Logic:
1. Attack with direct hits, 5 slow hits.
2. Attack in half-arc, 4 slow hits.
3. Attack in arc, 3 slow hits.
4. Wait a second
5. Explosion (three arc hits)
6. Wait a couple of seconds, and repeat
